Here is a sample application for a sick leave:

[Your Name]
[Your Address]
[City, State, ZIP]
[Date]

[Recipient's Name]
[Recipient's Title]
[Company/Organization Name]
[Company/Organization Address]
[City, State, ZIP]

Dear [Recipient's Name],

I am writing to inform you that I will be unable to come to work for [number] days due to illness. I am currently under medical care and my doctor has advised me to take a break from work to recover.

I am requesting sick leave from [start date] to [end date]. I apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and will make sure to catch up on any missed work as soon as I am able to return.

I have attached a medical certificate from my doctor, which confirms my illness and the recommended duration of my leave. If there is any additional documentation required, please let me know and I will provide it promptly.

I appreciate your understanding and approval of my sick leave application. If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact me.

Thank you for your support and consideration.

Sincerely,

[Your Signature]
[Your Name]

Attachments: Medical Certificate

Note: You can modify this sample application to fit your specific needs and circumstances. Be sure to include the following information:

* Your name and contact information
* The date of your application
* The recipient's name and title
* The company/organization name and address
* The reason for your leave (illness)
* The duration of your leave
* Any supporting documentation (medical certificate)
* Your signature and date
