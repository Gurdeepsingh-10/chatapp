Here is an email template for a salary raise request:

Subject: Request for Salary Raise

Dear [Manager's Name],

I hope this email finds you well. I wanted to take a moment to discuss my current salary with you. Over the past [X] months/years, I have been dedicated to my role as [Position] at [Company Name], consistently delivering high-quality work and taking on additional responsibilities to contribute to the growth and success of the company.

I have been with [Company Name] for [X] years and have consistently received positive performance reviews. I have successfully [briefly mention a few of your key accomplishments and how they have positively impacted the company]. I believe that my hard work and dedication have earned me a salary increase, and I would like to request a raise of 5% to my current annual salary.

This increase will not only be a well-deserved recognition of my efforts but also a motivation to continue delivering exceptional results. I believe that this salary adjustment is reasonable and reflects the value I bring to the company. I would appreciate it if we could schedule a meeting to discuss this request further.

My current salary is $ [Current Salary], and I am requesting an increase to $ [New Salary]. I am confident that we can work together to find a solution that benefits both me and the company.

If you require any additional information or would like to schedule a meeting, please let me know. I appreciate your time and consideration, and I look forward to hearing from you soon.

Thank you for your understanding and support.

Best regards,

[Your Name]

CC: [HR Email, if applicable]

Note: Before sending the email, make sure to customize it to fit your specific situation and company culture. Also, be prepared to discuss your request and provide evidence of your accomplishments during the meeting.
